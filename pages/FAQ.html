FAQS

AT THE FESTIVAL

Will Call is located at the main entrance gates—just follow the appropriate signs. Have your I.D. ready when picking up. Gate workers will match your ID to the appropriate order.
Gate times are from 5.30 PM to 04.00 AM daily.
Prohibited items include weapons, outside food and drinks, illegal substances, and large backpacks.
Yes, foldable chairs are allowed in the designated seating areas.

CAMPING

You can contact the water service team at the campground office.
Yes, fresh water stations are available near the shower areas.
Campfires are only allowed in designated fire pits and must be extinguished after use.
Pets are not allowed in the camping area, with the exception of service animals.

PARKING

General parking is available at the north and south lots of the venue. VIP parking requires a pass.
Yes, there is a designated area for motorcycle parking near the south entrance.
Yes, EV charging stations are available in the main parking lot. Please ensure your vehicle is compatible with our chargers.
Parking lots open 2 hours before the gates open each day.

Contact us: info@metalfest2025.com
